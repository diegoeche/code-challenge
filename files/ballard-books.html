About

Description

James Graham Ballard was an English novelist and short-story writer, satirist and essayist known for psychologically provocative works of fiction that explore the relations between human psychology, technology, sex and mass media. Wikipedia
Born: November 15, 1930, Shanghai, China
Died: April 19, 2009 (age 78 years), London, United Kingdom
Influenced by: William S. Burroughs, Sigmund Freud, Franz Kafka, Marshall McLuhan, Jean Genet, Joseph Heller
Children: Bea Ballard, Fay Ballard, James Ballard
Parents: James Ballard, Edna Johnstone
