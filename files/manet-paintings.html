Description

Édouard Manet was a French modernist painter. He was one of the first 19th-century artists to paint modern life, as well as a pivotal figure in the transition from Realism to Impressionism. Wikipedia
Born: January 23, 1832, Paris, France
Died: April 30, 1883 (age 51 years), Paris, France
Periods: Impressionism, Realism, Modern art, Modernism
Influenced by: Francisco Goya, Eugène Delacroix, Diego Velázquez, Thomas Couture
Spouse: Suzanne Manet (m. 1863–1883)
Children: Léon Leenhoff
